Nodira Bekmatova
PhD Candidate | English Philologist | Senior Educator
I am a Senior English Educator with over 16 years of professional experience, currently serving as a Coordinator for district CPD action plans. My doctoral research focuses on the structural-semantic analysis of Information Technology (IT) lexical units.

My work bridges the gap between traditional philology and Natural Language Processing (NLP), specifically investigating the hierarchical organization and semantic shifts of technical lexis. I am dedicated to developing data-driven frameworks for English for Specific Purposes (ESP) by integrating AI and digital gamification tools into pedagogical practice.
