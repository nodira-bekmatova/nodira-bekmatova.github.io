# Nodira Bekmatova
## PhD Candidate | English Philologist | Senior Educator

As an English Philologist and Educator with over **16 years of professional experience**, my doctoral research focuses on the **structural-semantic analysis of Information Technology (IT) lexical units**. 

My work bridges the gap between traditional philology and **Natural Language Processing (NLP)**, specifically investigating the hierarchical organization and semantic shifts of technical lexis. I am currently dedicated to developing data-driven frameworks for **English for Specific Purposes (ESP)** by integrating **Distributional Semantics** and **Generative AI** into pedagogical practice.

### Research Interests
*   Lexical Structural Analysis
*   Corpus Linguistics & NLP
*   Digital Pedagogy & AI in Language Education
*   English for Academic Purposes (EAP)
